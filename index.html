HighwaySurfer – Account Deletion Guide

If you no longer wish to use HighwaySurfer, you can permanently delete your account by following the steps below. Please note that this action is irreversible.

Step 1: Open the game Highway Surfer and go to the main menu screen.
Step 2: At the top center of the screen, tap the Account Icon.
Step 3: The Account popup will appear showing your Username.
Step 4: Tap the Delete Account button.
Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data and progress will be erased once the account is deleted.
